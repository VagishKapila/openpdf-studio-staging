Why Firms Choose ACH

When clients pay by credit card, they can dispute the charge up to 120 days later — even after you've completed the work. ACH bank transfers dramatically reduce this risk. Disputes are rare, the burden of proof falls on the client, and the dispute window is much shorter.

Save $107+ per transaction vs credit card

On a $4,000 payment, your firm pays just $25 in total fees with ACH compared to $132.40 with a credit card. Lower fees, lower risk, faster certainty.

All payments are securely processed through Stripe, a PCI Level 1 certified payment processor. Failed ACH payments incur a $4 Stripe fee. Disputed payments incur a $15 Stripe dispute fee regardless of payment method. Monthly account fees are only charged during months when at least one payout is issued. Instant payouts are available to eligible US debit cards and bank accounts. Standard payout timing is 2 business days. All fees are subject to change with 30 days notice. International card transactions may incur an additional 1.5% fee.
